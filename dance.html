Bollywood Dance Class:

The term ‘Bollywood’ comes from the combination of the names of Bombay (now known as Mumbai) and Hollywood. The Bollywood film industry, based in Mumbai, is the largest film industry in the world in terms of ticket sales and the amount of films made per year.Bollywood films are mostly musicals with very few movies made without at least one song and dance sequence.

We believe "Bollywood" is not just one specific style of dance, but a collage of many dance styles set to chart-topping Bollywood songs. Traditionally, Bollywood dance has been associated with the 'hip-shaking', 'light-bulb twisting' types of moves.

We use the best songs from the film industry, Each song has it's own feel and vibe. We create routines which are connected and appropriate to truly interpret each song.

Class Age Groups:

We offer classes for the following ages: 3 & 4 year olds, 5-8 year olds, 12-15 year olds and 15 and Adults (ages 15+ are welcome in any adults class).

Class Levels:

  • Basic:

    • The Basic class introduces students to fundamental steps, techniques and styles in Bollywood dance along with an emphasis on posture, alignment, strength, flexibility and coordination. There is a focus on performance - as expressions and emotions are intrinsic to Bollywood. Gaining self-confidence is an important aspect and goal of this level. All new students are required to enroll in a Basic class as a starting point in the dance program. Promotions to higher level classes are based on instructor approval.

  • Intermediate:

    • The Intermediate class consists of intricate steps and choreography along with a greater emphasis on the performance aspect of dance. The skills studied in the Basic level are further honed; and more complex elements such as prop work, floor routines, turns, formations and partner work are explored. To enroll in an intermediate class, the student must have mastered all the fundamental steps taught in the Basic class and be promoted by the instructor.

  • Advanced:

    • The Advanced level class engage students by combining various styles and steps in challenging combinations. Bollywood at this level becomes more of a fusion of styles. The emphasis on stage theatrics is highlighted so each student can further develop her/his individual performance skills. Clear and precise execution becomes the key. To enroll in an Advanced level class, the student must have mastered all the steps taught in the Intermediate class and be promoted by the instructor.
